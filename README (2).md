## ğŸ”¬ How to Test Your Water

HydroWatch is designed to work alongside a simple home or classroom experiment:

1. Collect a 90mL sample of your water source
2. Mix with a salt or sugar density separation solution
3. Filter through a 20-micron or finer filter
4. Let dry, then count particles under magnification
5. Submit your results to the global map!

Full protocol available inside the app under the **Research Planner** tab.
